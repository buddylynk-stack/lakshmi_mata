About Buddylynk

Our Mission

Buddylynk is on a mission to create meaningful connections in a digital world. We believe social networking should bring people together, not divide them.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Buddylynk was created to be a better social network - one that respects privacy, promotes genuine interactions, and helps people stay connected with those who matter most.

What Makes Us Different

  • Privacy-first approach - your data is yours
  • No intrusive ads interrupting your experience
  • AI-powered content moderation for a safe community
  • Real-time messaging and group chats
  • Photo and video sharing made easy
