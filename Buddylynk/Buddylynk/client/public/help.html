Buddylynk Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I create an account? Visit buddylynk.com/register and sign up with your email or social login.

How do I reset my password? Click "Forgot Password" on the login page.

Using Buddylynk

How do I share photos? Click the + button, select media, add caption, and post.

How do I message friends? Go to Chat and start a conversation.

How do I join groups? Browse Groups and click Join on any community.
