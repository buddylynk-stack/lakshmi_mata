Safety Policy

Suicide, Self-Injury, and Eating Disorders Policy

Last Updated: November 24, 2025

1. Purpose

Buddylynk is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users. This policy outlines how Buddylynk manages content and behavior related to suicide, self-harm, self-injury, and eating disorders. Content that encourages, promotes, or facilitates such harm is strictly prohibited.

2. Prohibited Content

Users may not create, upload, share, or engage in content that includes:

A. Promotion or Encouragement of Harm

  • Promotion or encouragement of suicide or self-harm
  • Instructions, methods, or techniques for suicide or self-injury
  • Promotion of eating disorders, including starvation, purging, or extreme dieting advice intended to cause harm

B. Graphic Content

  • Graphic images or videos depicting self-inflicted injuries
  • Content showing or describing self-harm in a way intended to shock, provoke, or encourage harmful behavior

C. Dangerous Challenges or Activities

  • Participation in or encouragement of harmful challenges
  • Requests or dares that involve self-injury or severe dieting practices

3. Restricted Content

Some content related to mental health may be allowed under strict conditions. Content that discusses suicide, self-harm, or eating disorders for educational, supportive, or recovery-based purposes is permitted only if:

  • The content discourages harmful behavior
  • The content encourages users to seek help
  • No instructions or harmful encouragement are included
  • No graphic or triggering imagery is present

Buddylynk may apply warnings or limit visibility of such content.

4. Supportive Content

Buddylynk allows content that:

  • Encourages seeking medical or mental health support
  • Shares personal recovery stories without graphic descriptions
  • Provides resources for crisis intervention
  • Educates users on mental health awareness

All supportive content must avoid details that could enable harmful behavior.

5. Platform Actions

Buddylynk may take any of the following actions if content violates this policy:

  • Immediate removal of violating content
  • Account warnings
  • Temporary or permanent account suspension
  • Reporting imminent threats to appropriate authorities if legally required

Buddylynk reserves the right to escalate any situation where an individual appears to be in immediate danger.

6. User Reporting

Users who encounter content involving suicide, self-injury, or eating disorders should report it immediately using Buddylynk's reporting tools. Buddylynk reviews such reports with priority and may take action based on the severity of the situation.

7. Crisis Resources

Buddylynk may display crisis hotline information or mental health resources when:

  • A user searches for terms related to self-harm
  • A user posts content signaling immediate risk
  • A report indicates that a user may need urgent support

Buddylynk encourages users to seek professional help in emergency situations.

Emergency Resources

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988

Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741

National Eating Disorders Association: 1-800-931-2237

Emergency Services: 911

8. No Medical Advice

Buddylynk does not provide medical, psychological, or therapeutic advice. Any information on the platform should not be used as a substitute for professional care. Users are responsible for seeking qualified help when needed.

9. Enforcement

Buddylynk reserves full discretion in enforcing this policy. We may update this section as required to address new risks, legal requirements, or safety concerns.
