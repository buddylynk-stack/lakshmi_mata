Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

Last Updated: November 24, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Buddylynk ("we," "our," or "the platform") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when you use our services. By creating an account or accessing Buddylynk, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

We may collect personal information you submit directly, including:

  • Full name, username, and profile details
  • Email address or phone number
  • Date of birth (if required)
  • Posts, comments, messages, and user-generated content
  • Photos, videos, and uploaded media

B. Information Collected Automatically

When you use Buddylynk, we automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Device type, operating system, and browser
  • Activity logs and usage analytics
  • Cookies and tracking data
  • Approximate location data based on IP address

C. Optional Information

With your consent, we may collect:

  • Contact list for friend connection features
  • Additional profile information you choose to provide

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for operational, security, and service-related purposes, including:

  • Account creation and management
  • Personalization of content and recommendations
  • Service optimization and platform improvement
  • Delivery of notifications and security alerts
  • Prevention of fraud, abuse, and platform misuse
  • Technical and Customer Care support

We do not sell personal information.

4. How We Share Information

A. Sharing With Other Users

Certain information is visible to other users, such as:

  • Public posts
  • Comments
  • Profile information you choose to display

B. Service Providers

We may share data with third-party service providers that assist with:

  • Hosting infrastructure
  • Analytics
  • Payment processing (if applicable)

All service providers are required to protect data and use it only for contractual purposes.

C. Legal Compliance

We may disclose information to comply with:

  • Applicable laws
  • Law enforcement requests
  • Court orders
  • Threats to safety, security, or rights of the platform or users

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Buddylynk uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain user sessions
  • Improve site performance
  • Personalize user experience

Users may disable cookies in their browser settings; however, some features may become limited or unavailable.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct or update inaccurate information
  • Request account deletion
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Adjust privacy and visibility settings

All requests can be submitted to: customercare@buddylynk.com

7. Data Retention

Buddylynk retains personal information for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, personal data is removed or anonymized unless retention is required by law.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard safeguards to protect stored data, including:

  • Encryption
  • Access controls
  • Secure servers
  • Monitoring for suspicious or unauthorized activity

Although we employ protective measures, no system is guaranteed to be completely secure. We continue to improve our security processes and protocols.

9. Children's Privacy

Buddylynk does not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, contact us at customercare@buddylynk.com, and we will promptly remove the account and associated data.

10. International Data Transfers

Buddylynk is operated and managed in the United States, specifically under the jurisdiction of Virginia. By using the platform, you acknowledge that your personal information may be stored, processed, or transferred within the United States. We take appropriate steps to ensure data handled within Virginia complies with applicable privacy and data protection laws.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in legal, technical, or operational requirements. Revisions will be published on this page, and the "Last Updated" date will be modified accordingly. Significant changes may be communicated directly to users.

12. Contact Information

For questions, requests, or concerns related to privacy, contact:

Customer Care, Buddylynk

Email: customercare@buddylynk.com

Website: www.buddylynk.com
